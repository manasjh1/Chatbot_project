### Course Structure for M.Tech. in ECE
**Specialization:** Railway Signalling, Telecommunication, and RAMS  
**Duration:** 2 Years  
**Batch:** 2024-2026

**University:** Gautam Buddha University, Greater Noida, U.P., India  
**Department:** Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)  
**School:** Information and Communication Technology  

This document provides the semester-wise structure for the **M.Tech. in ECE** program specializing in **Railway Signalling, Telecommunication, and RAMS** for the **batch 2024-2026**.

---

## **Semester I (Session 2024-2026)**

1. **ER101:** Basics of Signalling Engineering *(3-1-0)* - **4 Credits**
2. **ER103:** Basics of Railway Engineering *(3-0-0)* - **3 Credits**
3. **ER105:** Basics of Telecom Engineering *(3-0-0)* - **3 Credits**
4. **EC401/EE571/CS401:** Optical Communication / Power System Analysis and Control / Compiler Design *(3-0-0)* - **3 Credits**
5. **Elective 1:** *(3-0-0)* - **3 Credits**
6. **EC481/EE591/CS481:** Optical Communication Lab / Power System Lab / Compiler Design Lab *(0-0-3)* - **2 Credits**
7. **ER001:** System Design Lab *(0-0-2)* - **1 Credit**
8. **EC493/EE493/CS493:** Minor Project *(0-0-8)* - **4 Credits**

**Total Credits:** 23
