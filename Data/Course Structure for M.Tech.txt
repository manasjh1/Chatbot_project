## **Detailed Course Descriptions**

### **Digital IC Design (EC535)**

- **Unit I:** Introduction to digital logic, Boolean algebra, CMOS implementation, and circuit design.
- **Unit II:** Combinational Logic Design, multiplexers, decoders, PLDs.
- **Unit III:** Sequential Circuits using Flip-Flops and state machines.
- **Unit IV:** Synchronous design with programmable devices.
- **Unit V:** System design using HDL.

**Textbooks:**

- C.H. Roth Jr., *Fundamentals of Logic Design*.
- J. Bhaskar, *A Verilog Primer*.

---

### **Wireless Networks (EC540)**

- **Unit I:** Cellular fundamentals, SIR calculations, and mobile data networks.
- **Unit II:** Multiple Access techniques (TDMA, FDMA, CDMA).
- **Unit III:** WLAN standards (802.11), TCP over wireless.
- **Unit IV:** WPAN and geolocation systems.
- **Unit V:** Security and privacy in wireless networks.

**Textbooks:**

- W. Stallings, *Wireless Communications and Networks*.
- K. Pahlavan, *Principles of Wireless Networks*.

---

### **Mobile Computing (EC635)**

- **Unit I:** Mobile computing architecture, GSM.
- **Unit II:** Client programming and platform development.
- **Unit III:** Programming for Symbian OS and Palm OS.
- **Unit IV:** Wireless application protocols and Bluetooth.
- **Unit V:** Emerging trends like m-commerce.

**Textbooks:**

- A.K. Talukder, *Mobile Computing*.
- J. Schiller, *Mobile Communications*.

---

### **Security in Wireless Networks (EC631)**

- **Unit I:** Security goals and threats.
- **Unit II:** Symmetric key ciphers.
- **Unit III:** Modern cryptographic methods.
- **Unit IV:** Wireless LAN security.
- **Unit V:** VPNs and access control.

**Textbook:**

- B.A. Forouzan, *Cryptography & Network Security*.

---

### **Dissertation and Lab Work**

The dissertation projects in **M.Tech. in ICT** for **Wireless Communication and Networks** require students to work on industry-relevant problems, implement wireless network protocols, and contribute to research publications.

\*\*This course structure applies exclusively to the **M.Tech. in ICT** program specializing in **Wireless Communication and Networks** for the **session 2024-2026**.
